About Us

In the heart of a small, picturesque town nestled between rolling hills and serene countryside, HarmoniGuitars was born. Founded in 1975 by two lifelong friends, Daniel Archer and Benjamin Harper, this company has become a symbol of passion, craftsmanship, and a deep love for music.

The name "HarmoniGuitars" was inspired by their shared vision of creating instruments that would produce harmonious and soul-stirring sounds. They set out to create guitars that would blend traditional craftsmanship with innovative designs, catering to a wide range of musicians, from budding beginners to seasoned professionals.

Daniel Archer

Daniel Archer, a co-founder of HarmoniGuitars, was born in 1950 with a profound love for music coursing through his veins. Raised in the same picturesque town where HarmoniGuitars would later be established, he discovered his passion for guitars at a young age. Daniel's exceptional skills as a craftsman and his innate musical talent made him the driving force behind the company's guitar designs.

With an insatiable curiosity, Daniel tirelessly experimented with various materials and construction techniques, seeking to create instruments that not only looked beautiful but also produced enchanting sounds. His commitment to quality and innovation laid the foundation for HarmoniGuitars' success. Despite the company's global recognition, Daniel remained grounded, ever the music enthusiast, pouring his heart and soul into every guitar he crafted.

Benjamin Harper

Benjamin Harper, the other half of HarmoniGuitars' founding duo, was born in 1952, just a stone's throw away from Daniel's childhood home. Like Daniel, Benjamin's love for music was a lifelong affair. His artistic sensibility and keen eye for detail complemented Daniel's craftsmanship perfectly. Benjamin was responsible for the exquisite designs and aesthetics that set HarmoniGuitars apart.

His artistic touch could turn a simple piece of wood into a work of art that not only sounded divine but was visually captivating. Benjamin's dedication to making each HarmoniGuitar a unique masterpiece made the instruments cherished by musicians and collectors alike. His harmonious blend of artistry and practicality played a pivotal role in shaping the company's identity.

Colin Macklin

Colin Macklin, a close friend and trusted collaborator of Daniel and Benjamin, joined the HarmoniGuitars team in the late 1970s. Born in 1954, Colin brought a different dimension to the company. His expertise in woodworking and technical craftsmanship complemented the founders' vision, allowing them to take their guitars to new heights.

Colin was the wizard behind the scenes, the one who ensured that every technical detail of the guitars was finely tuned to perfection. His meticulous attention to detail and unwavering commitment to quality control played a crucial role in the company's ability to produce instruments that were not only beautiful but also reliable and consistent in their performance.
